About Us

Our expert truck dispatchers connect you directly with trusted brokers, so you can focus on driving. We handle all the necessary paperwork and follow up on lumper and detention pay, taking the hassle out of the process. Our dispatchers take care of all administrative tasks, and you can choose your preferred driving regions. We negotiate to get you the best rates and routes. By working closely with top shippers and brokers, we ensure you get paid on time. Your dedicated dispatcher finds the best loads for dry freight, flatbeds, and reefers, keeping your trucks on the road and reducing downtime. At Express Truckers, we make sure you stay active and profitable.
